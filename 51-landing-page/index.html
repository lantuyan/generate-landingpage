MANIFESTO

"THE WORKER WHO OWNS THEIR WORKPLACE
IS A WORKER WHO IS FREE."

We believe that those who labor should own the fruits of their labor. For too long, workers have been separated from the means of production, their creativity extracted, their dignity diminished.

KOLLEKTIV exists to change this. We are building the infrastructure for economic democracy—connecting workers with cooperative opportunities, providing tools for collective organization, and proving that another economy is possible.

COLLECTIVE OWNERSHIP

Workers own the businesses they build. Profits shared, decisions made together.

SOLIDARITY NETWORKS

Connected cooperatives supporting each other across industries and borders.

DEMOCRATIC POWER

One worker, one vote. Leadership elected, accountability ensured.
