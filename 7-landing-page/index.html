Our Philosophy

Finding beauty
in simplicity

Wabi-Sabi

We embrace imperfection as a form of beauty. Each piece carries the authentic marks of handcraft—subtle variations that tell stories of human touch.

Hygge

Creating spaces that nurture warmth and togetherness. Our designs invite you to slow down, gather close, and find comfort in simplicity.

Sustainability

Earth-conscious by design. We source responsibly, craft mindfully, and create pieces meant to be treasured across generations.

The Process

Crafted with
reverence

Every Komorebi piece begins as a meditation. Our artisans—trained in both Japanese joinery and Scandinavian woodworking traditions—approach each creation with the focused calm of a tea ceremony.

We work with sustainably harvested hardwoods, hand-spun textiles, and ceramics fired in small batches. No shortcuts. No compromise. Just honest craft that honors both maker and material.
