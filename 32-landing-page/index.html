The Journey

From Discovery to Revival

01

Initial Consultation

Share your piece with us through photos or an in-person visit. We'll assess its condition, authenticity, and restoration potential.

02

Expert Analysis

Our team examines construction details, materials, and provenance markers. You receive a comprehensive report with our findings and recommendations.

03

Meticulous Restoration

Using period-appropriate techniques and materials, we carefully restore your piece. Regular updates keep you informed throughout the process.

04

Authenticated Delivery

Your restored piece is returned with full documentation, authentication certificate, and care guidelines for future generations.

Our Story

Guardians of Design Heritage

Founded by master craftsmen who apprenticed under original Herman Miller artisans, Retrograde Atelier has spent three decades preserving the masterworks of mid-century modern design.

Our workshop combines traditional woodworking techniques with modern conservation science. Every piece that passes through our hands receives the same meticulous attention that its original creator intended.
