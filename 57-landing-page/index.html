Our Mission

Guardians of the Deep

The ocean covers 71% of our planet, yet only 8% is protected. OceanGuard empowers everyday people to become active participants in marine conservation.

Through our three-pillar approach—citizen science, sustainable seafood guidance, and volunteer cleanup programs—we create meaningful connections between communities and the seas that sustain us all.

Citizen Science

Collect vital data on marine life, water quality, and ecosystem health. Your observations contribute to real scientific research and policy decisions.

  • Whale & dolphin sighting reports
  • Beach biodiversity surveys
  • Water temperature monitoring
  • Microplastic sampling
