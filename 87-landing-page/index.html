Simple Process

How Co-Ownership Works

We've simplified the path to vacation home ownership. From finding your perfect property to enjoying your time there, we manage every detail.

Find Your Property

Browse our curated collection of vacation homes in sought-after destinations. Each property is vetted for quality, location, and investment potential.

Form Your Group

Invite friends or family to co-own with you, or join an existing group of like-minded owners. We help match compatible ownership partners.

Legal Structure

We establish a proper LLC structure, handle all legal documentation, and create fair usage agreements that protect everyone's interests.

Enjoy Ownership

Access our scheduling platform, enjoy hassle-free maintenance, and create lasting memories. When ready, we manage the sale process too.

Why CoOwn

Smart Investment,
Shared Memories

Vacation home ownership has traditionally been out of reach for most. CoOwn changes that by making shared ownership simple, legal, and enjoyable.

  • Fraction of the Cost

    Own 1/4 to 1/8 of a luxury property, paying only your share of purchase and maintenance costs.

  • Professional Management

    From cleaning to repairs to landscaping—we handle all property management so you simply enjoy.

  • Smart Scheduling

    Our fair scheduling system ensures everyone gets prime time access. Swap dates easily with co-owners.

  • Legal Protection

    Proper LLC structure and comprehensive agreements protect your investment and define clear ownership rules.
