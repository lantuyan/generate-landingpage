# Cinematic Subtitles Landing Page

The emotional architecture toggles between trust and curiosity, quiet and spectacle, expressed through warm grayscale with a sudden sunrise band—credits rolling into dawn.

Text appears at the lower third like a subway platform wayfinding system, with chaptered long-take scrolling that treats financial concepts as scenes in a narrative. Motion follows a museum-slow rhythm, numbers appearing with the patience of translation. Typography spacing creates the pause between reading and comprehension, the breath of processing meaning.

Reference anchors: The contemplative pacing of Tokyo's Daikanyama T-Site bookstore shapes the browsing temporality. The visual grammar of Yasujiro Ozu's pillow shots—quiet frames between action—informs the transitional moments. The craft of film title design, particularly Saul Bass's narrative typography, guides how text carries story weight.
