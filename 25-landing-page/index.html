Features

Everything creative teams need

Designed from the ground up for visual thinkers and creative professionals.

Visual Kanban

Drag-and-drop boards that make project status crystal clear. Customizable columns, labels, and swimlanes.

Asset Management

Store, organize, and version all your creative assets. Smart folders and instant search.

Real-time Collaboration

Work together seamlessly. Comments, mentions, and live cursors keep everyone in sync.

Task Automation

Automate repetitive workflows. Set rules, triggers, and actions to save hours every week.

Timeline View

See the big picture with Gantt-style timelines. Plan sprints, track milestones, and hit deadlines.

Team Workspaces

Dedicated spaces for each team or project. Fine-grained permissions and guest access.
