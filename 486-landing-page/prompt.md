# Negative Space Theatre Landing Page

A Negative Space Theatre aesthetic with silent comedy and precision fog stages this climate-adaptive home guidance platform. The emotional production balances gravity and lightness alongside serenity and urgency—sustainability as drama performed in absence. Two-tone duotone with one forbidden color: stage black and spotlight white interrupted by emergency amber.

Typography enters from emptiness, words appearing like actors into available light. Spacing creates wings and depth, theatrical dimensions enabling dramatic sustainability narrative. The scene cuts scroll choreography presents climate solutions as distinct theatrical moments, hard transitions maintaining dramatic impact. Motion carries paper-flip energy—elements turning like stage machinery revealing scenes. The theatre stage spatial metaphor transforms home guidance into environmental performance, each recommendation a dramatic beat.

Reference anchors: Oskar Schlemmer's Bauhaus theatre experiments; the Theatre of the Absurd's profound comedy; the tactile craft of stage set construction where illusion becomes real. These make climate action feel like important theatre.
