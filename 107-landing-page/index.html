How It Works

Simple tools for meaningful coordination

We've distilled years of community organizing wisdom into an intuitive platform that makes sharing feel natural.

01

Create Your Circle

Define your neighborhood boundaries and invite trusted neighbors. Start with as few as five households—communities grow organically.

02

Share Resources

List what you can share—tools, skills, time, space. Our matching helps neighbors find what they need without endless scrolling.

03

Coordinate Together

Built-in scheduling for carpools, rotating childcare, bulk order deadlines. Gentle reminders keep everyone in sync without overwhelm.

04

Build Trust

Every exchange strengthens community bonds. Our gratitude system celebrates contributions without gamifying relationships.

Community Models

Discover what's possible in your neighborhood

Every community is unique. Explore the coordination models that resonate with your neighborhood's needs and values.

Tool Library

Share rarely-used tools—drills, ladders, pressure washers—and save hundreds per household annually. Most tools sit unused 99% of their life.

Carpool Network

Coordinate school runs, commutes, and errands. Reduce carbon footprint while building relationships through shared journeys.

Childcare Co-op

Trade childcare hours with trusted neighbors. Flexible scheduling that adapts to real family life, not rigid programs.

Bulk Buying Group

Pool purchasing power for organic produce, household goods, local crafts. Access wholesale prices while supporting local producers.

Skill Exchange

Trade tutoring, music lessons, home repairs, gardening expertise. Every neighbor brings unique gifts to share.

Our Philosophy

Built on trust, not transactions

Time, Not Money

Communities thrive when exchanges happen through time and trust, not dollars. Our credit-free model keeps relationships genuine.

Privacy First

Your data stays local. We never sell information or track behavior. Communities own their coordination patterns.

Human Scale

Neighborhoods work best with 20-100 households. We help you stay connected without growing into impersonal networks.

Offline Ready

Real community happens face-to-face. We're the coordination layer, not the replacement for porch conversations.
