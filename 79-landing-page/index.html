Common Questions

We're happy to answer any questions you have

I've never used a smartphone before. Is that okay? +

Absolutely! Many of our clients have never touched a smartphone before their first session. We start from the very beginning‚Äîhow to turn it on, what the buttons do, and how to make your first call. There's no such thing as a "silly question" with us.

What if I forget what I learned? +

That's completely normal! We provide large-print, step-by-step written guides you can keep by your phone. Plus, our phone support line is always available if you get stuck. We expect to repeat things‚Äîthat's how learning works.

Do you come to my home? +

Yes! In-home sessions are available in our service area. Many clients prefer learning in their own comfortable environment, on their own devices. We can also meet at libraries, community centers, or conduct sessions via video call.

My vision isn't great. Can you still help me? +

Yes! We specialize in making technology accessible. We'll help you adjust text size, enable voice features, and set up your device to work best for your vision. Many accessibility features are built into phones‚Äîwe'll show you how to use them.

Can my family member join the session? +

Family members are always welcome! Sometimes it helps to have someone there for support. We can also provide progress updates to family members if you'd like them involved in your learning journey.
