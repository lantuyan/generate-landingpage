WHAT WE'RE ABOUT

✦

DIY OR DIE

We book our own shows, print our own posters, and answer to no one. Every penny goes back to the artists.

☆

ALL AGES ALWAYS

Music shouldn't have an age limit. If you're here for the music, you're in. No exceptions.

◈

SAFE SPACE

No racism. No sexism. No homophobia. No transpobia. No bullshit. This is a community, act like it.

"We started this because every venue in town sold out. Literally. Now they book cover bands and charge $15 for watered-down beer. We wanted a place where the music matters more than the margins."
— Marcus & Luna, Founders (written on a napkin, 2019)
