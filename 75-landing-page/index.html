The Process

Structured Path to Resolution

Our methodical approach ensures every step is transparent, fair, and purposefully designed to move disputes toward resolution.

01

Initial Consultation

Confidential assessment of your dispute. We analyze the nature of the conflict, parties involved, and recommend the most appropriate resolution pathway.

1-2 Business Days
02

Agreement & Scheduling

All parties sign the resolution agreement, select their preferred neutral, and establish the procedural framework and timeline.

3-5 Business Days
03

Document Exchange

Parties submit relevant documents and position statements through our secure platform. Limited, focused discovery keeps proceedings efficient.

1-2 Weeks
04

Resolution Sessions

Virtual or in-person proceedings conducted with professional rigor. Our neutrals guide discussions toward fair, practical outcomes.

1-3 Sessions
05

Final Resolution

Settlement agreement or arbitration award issued. Binding decisions are enforceable in courts under the Federal Arbitration Act.

Within 30 Days
