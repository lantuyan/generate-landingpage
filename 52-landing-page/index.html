Pure Form.
Absolute Function.

GRIDFORM is a revolutionary modular furniture system built on the principles of De Stijl—the art movement that reduced design to its essential elements. Every piece follows the logic of horizontal and vertical lines, primary colors, and asymmetric balance.

Our intuitive online configurator lets you become the architect of your own living space, combining modules that follow the same geometric harmony as a Mondrian masterpiece.
