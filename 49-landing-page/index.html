Our Heritage

A Legacy Rooted in Petals

For over a century, the Floraison atelier has cultivated the art of botanical perfumery in the verdant valleys where rare flowers bloom under morning mists. Our founders believed that true fragrance cannot be hurried—it must unfurl naturally, like petals greeting the dawn.

Each generation has passed down the sacred techniques: the patience to harvest at perfect bloom, the wisdom to extract without diminishing, the artistry to compose scents that speak to the soul. We work only with flowers grown in harmony with the land, ensuring that our fragrances carry not just beauty, but integrity.

The Craft

From Petal to Perfume

I

Cultivation

Our gardens bloom with rare botanicals—Florentine iris, Bulgarian roses, night-blooming jasmine—each tended by hand in soil enriched over generations.

II

Harvest

At dawn, when essential oils concentrate in the petals, our artisans harvest by hand—each flower cut at its moment of perfection.

III

Distillation

In copper alembics passed down through generations, we coax essence from petal through gentle steam—never rushing, never forcing.

IV

Composition

Our master perfumer blends essences in ratios refined over a century—each formula a family secret, each bottle a work of living art.
