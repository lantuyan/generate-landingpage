/* ABOUT */

"The average lifespan of a webpage is 100 days."
— Internet Archive Studies, 2024

ARCHIVE.RAW is a digital preservation service for researchers, historians, journalists, and organizations who understand that the internet is not permanent.

We operate on a simple principle: function over form. Our infrastructure captures websites exactly as they appear—HTML, CSS, JavaScript, media, and all dependencies. No beautification. No interpretation. Just raw, authentic digital artifacts.

FOUNDED: 2019
HEADQUARTERS: Distributed (No single point of failure)
STORAGE: Petabyte-scale, geographically redundant
PHILOSOPHY: Honest. Raw. Functional.
