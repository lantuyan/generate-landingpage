# Dark Mode Luxury with Japanese Mono-ha Landing Page

The emotional arc flows: first impression of rare luxury → curiosity about sourcing regions → trust through provenance depth → desire for exceptional tea → clarity of subscription options → commitment to tea journey.

Reference **high-end atelier craftsmanship** suggesting tea worthy of ceremony. These abstractions should influence a landing page that feels luxuriously dark, philosophically grounded, and connoisseur-serving—a single scrolling experience where rare tea becomes accessible to serious enthusiasts.
